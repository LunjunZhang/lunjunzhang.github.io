Lunjun Zhang

I am a CS PhD student in the Machine Learning Group at University of Toronto advised by Professor Jimmy Ba.

I am also a student researcher at Google DeepMind working on Large Language Models.

From 2021 to 2024, I worked as a researcher at the self-driving startup Waabi. From 2017 to 2021, I studied Engineering Science at University of Toronto, and interned at Vector Institute, Mila, and Uber Advanced Technologies Group.

Contact: Email / Google Scholar / Github / Twitter

Research

I am broadly interested in building intelligent agents that can act in digital and physical worlds.

  • Recursive self improvement from weaker forms of feedback
  • Methods that can reliably improve planning and reasoning capabilities
  • More efficient unsupervised learning and reinforcement learning algorithms
